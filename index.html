About the Program

The M.Sc in Medical Physics at IIT Hyderabad is a rigorous, interdisciplinary program designed to train the next generation of leaders in medical physics. Our curriculum blends advanced physics, clinical applications, and hands-on research, in collaboration with leading hospitals and research institutes.

The program offers students access to state-of-the-art laboratories, clinical training, and research opportunities with international faculty. Graduates have a 100% placement record in hospitals, research labs, and academia.

Vision

To be a global leader in medical physics education, research, and innovation, contributing to advancements in healthcare and human well-being.

Mission

To provide world-class education and research opportunities in medical physics, foster interdisciplinary collaboration, and develop skilled professionals who drive progress in healthcare technology.
